\hypertarget{world-championship-wrestling}{%
\subsection{World Championship
Wrestling}\label{world-championship-wrestling}}

\begin{itemize}
\tightlist
\item
  Successfully feuded with Booker T, elevating his singles career.
\item
  Defenestrated on national television by Perry Saturn. It was
  technically a glass door, but I love the term ``defenestration'' and I
  don't get to use it much so I'm rolling with that.
\item
  Career effectively ended at Superbrawl VIII (:()
\end{itemize}

\hypertarget{world-wrestling-federation}{%
\subsection{World Wrestling
Federation}\label{world-wrestling-federation}}

\begin{itemize}
\tightlist
\item
  Runner-up in 1993 Intercontinental Championship Battle Royal (to Razor
  Ramon).
\item
  Wrestled Shawn Michaels to a countout draw in front of 80,000 fans in
  Wembley Stadium at Summerslam 1992.
\item
  Carried Tito Santana like dead weight to tag team prominence, before
  \href{https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VKnWAxg4R68}{tiring of him at
  Wrestlemania V}.
\item
  Had two amazing entrance themes where somehow the least amazing of the
  two is \href{https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1bNr3144GDI}{the one done
  by Robbie Dupree}.
\item
  Successfully cultivated ``The Model'' persona, relocating to Cocoa
  Beach for a gimmick that was years ahead of its time.
\item
  Launched successful line of cologne, \emph{Arrogance}, and advertised
  it in \href{https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zm9AbH7CnBc}{some amazing
  vignettes}.
\item
  \href{https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cs7sxJboGWA}{Temporarily blinded
  Jake ``The Snake'' Roberts}, incapacitating him for the better part of
  1990.
\item
  Successfully teamed with Tony Garea and briefly with Tom Zenk before
  Tom Zenk got apparently got weird about pay disparities.
\end{itemize}

\hypertarget{american-wrestling-association}{%
\subsection{American Wrestling
Association}\label{american-wrestling-association}}

\begin{itemize}
\tightlist
\item
  \href{https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=c3O_jGZd6EI}{Beat Jumbo goddamn
  Tsuruta for AWA World Heavyweight Championship}.
\item
  I'm going to make that another entry because seriously how cool is
  Jumbo as a proverbial notch on the belt? Jumbo Tsuruta was \emph{the}
  Japanese pro-wrestler of the 1980s.
\item
  Wrestled several matches with then-NWA World Champion Ric Flair.
\end{itemize}

\hypertarget{other-territories}{%
\subsection{Other Territories}\label{other-territories}}

\begin{itemize}
\tightlist
\item
  Emerged as top talent in the Pacific Northwest territory for the NWA.
\item
  Successfully teamed with Roddy Piper, winning a total of four tag team
  championships.
\item
  Feature attraction in multiple territories in Canada (e.g.~Stampede
  Wrestling), Puerto Rico (World Wrestling Council), and the United
  States (e.g.~Georgia Championship Wrestling)
\end{itemize}
